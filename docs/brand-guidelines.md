Brand Guidelines — Publiora

> Revision note — MVP typography default: Default heading font is Plus Jakarta Sans. Default body font is Inter. Optional alternatives: Sora, General Sans, and Switzer.

Tagline

\> Create, publish, and distribute marketing ebooks with AI.

---

1\. Brand Overview

Brand Positioning

Publiora adalah AI-powered publishing platform untuk creator dan marketer.

Bukan sekadar:

AI writer

PDF generator

chatbot

Publiora membantu user:

berpikir

menyusun strategi

membuat ebook

mempublish

mendistribusikan ebook marketing secara modern

---

Brand Essence

\> Elegant AI publishing for modern creators.

---

Brand Personality

1\. Elegant

Minimalis. Premium. Tenang. Tidak norak AI.

---

2\. Strategic

AI terasa pintar dan membantu berpikir.

---

3\. Modern

Clean SaaS aesthetic. Editorial feel.

---

4\. Creative

Mendorong ide dan kreativitas.

---

5\. Professional

Terlihat serius dan trustworthy.

---

Brand Feeling

User harus merasa:

“I’m building professional publishing assets.”

Bukan:

“I’m using an AI content spinner.”

---

---

Body Font

Recommended

Inter

Switzer

Characteristics:

Gunakan:

bold

oversized

strong hierarchy

72px
