Mitigation:

strategist UX

enhancement system

---

Risk 2

Mitigation:

superior reading UX

easier access

updateable content

---

Risk 3

Competitors copy generation features.

Mitigation:

focus ecosystem

focus workflow

focus distribution

---

Risk 4

Marketplace launched too early.

Mitigation:

build retention first

validate creator usage first
