PRD MVP — Publiora

Product Name

Publiora

Tagline

> Create, publish, and distribute marketing ebooks with AI.




---

TL;DR

Publiora adalah platform AI-powered publishing yang membantu creator, marketer, affiliate marketer, coach, dan digital product seller untuk:

brainstorming ebook

generate ebook marketing-ready

publish ebook online

distribute ebook via claim links

memberikan akses ebook kepada customer tanpa kirim PDF manual


Publiora menggunakan:

AI conversation strategist

structured ebook generation

HTML-first rendering

hosted ebook library

instant reader access system


MVP fokus pada:

1. AI Ebook Generator


2. Hosted Ebook Reader


3. Claim Link Access System


4. PDF Export


5. Creator Dashboard




---

Product Vision

Menjadi platform modern untuk:

> membuat, menerbitkan, dan mendistribusikan ebook marketing dengan AI.
